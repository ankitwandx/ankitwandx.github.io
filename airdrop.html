Get a *FREE* EOS account + 100 MUR tokens with Murmur.

A new age micro-blogging platform, Murmur is the place for peer-driven content and conversations, only more open, fully decentralized, and user-rewarding.

To participate in the airdrop

  1. Download Murmur on your device: Android or iOS

  2. Click ‘create a new EOS account’ and follow the steps prompted. If you are among the next 10000 participants, you won’t have to pay for EOS RAM* and get the EOS account for free.

  3. Once your account is activated (within 72hrs of registration submission), log in and refer Murmur to at least 2 friends .

  4. Actively publish for at least 30 days and get rewarded at the end of Day 30 with the 100 MUR Tokens. You also gain 40 MUR for every referral (up to 3 referrals allowed).

  5. Like Murmur on Facebook and join the Murmur Telegram group

Note

  1. The nature of the blockchain is such that creating an account on EOS requires one to pay for RAM upfront. Murmur will bear the cost of the RAM for the user, and also stake EOS for the user for them to be able to perform actions on Murmur

  2. You can connect an existing EOS account for using Murmur. If you choose to do so, you will be prompted to import your private key. Please note that Murmur does not store your private key and you are always in control of it. If you opt to use own EOS account, you will receive a reward of 300 MUR tokens in your Murmur wallet.

  3. Despite having an existing EOS account, you can request us to stake EOS for you. If you choose to do so, we’ll take 72 hrs to stake EOS.

  4. Existing app users can refer their friends and stand to receive 40 MUR for each referral (upto 3 referrals allowed). Those who install the app using an existing user’s referral code can follow the steps above to create/import EOS account and win MUR rewards (100 MUR for creating an account on Murmur, 300 MUR for importing an existing account)
